Job Interview

Competitors in this event will be competing for an entry level marketing position. This is an exciting position in Marketing so do not miss this opportunity to apply now! On Monday, September 16, 2013 download the job posting and start to prepare for your interview. A maximum of 20 minutes is allowed for the interview and be sure to bring your portfolio and extra copies of your resume. Our panel of judges will declare who acquires the position.

  • Download Job Description (PDF)
  • Download Rules and Regulations (PDF)

The Job Interview resumes and cover letter have a cut off date of Friday, November 1, 2013 by which time they must be postmarked for delivery to the Job Interview judge.
