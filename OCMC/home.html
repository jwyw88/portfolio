Welcome to OCMC 2013!

For more than three decades, Ontario colleges' business students have been taking part in a unique skills competition that tests their marketing mettle in a series of exercises that emulate real-world business challenges.

This year the competition will be held in beautiful downtown Windsor at the St. Clair College Centre for the Arts.

We are thrilled to welcome you to the southernmost college in Canada and experience our theme "The Heat Is On".

We can't wait to see you this November 21 and 22. The competition will be HOT, HOT, HOT!

See you at OCMC 2013 and get ready to sizzle!
