Awards

William & Margaret Lydiatt Award

The William & Margaret Lydiatt Award recognizes a student from a participating College who has demonstrated exceptional spirit in the weeks leading up to the OCMC.

Prior to the competition, each college should select a recipient of the Award. Determination of the recipient will include the following:

  • A student who supports others on the team and works exceptionally hard.
  • A student who puts the value and spirit of the competition by supporting others on the team, working hard and fostering growth in others.
  • A student who demonstrates commitment to the OCMC by such actions as recruiting others, working to promote or raise funds for the team or other actions.
  • A student who has overcome extraordinary adversity to get to the competition and who, by their example, inspires others.

Please use the Nomination Form found below, and send nominations and any attachments in word format only please:

Download The William & Margaret Lydiatt Nomination Form

Each college is to submit one copy of this form as an e-mail attachment to Nichole Rourke, Nrouke@stclaircollege.ca by Friday November 1, 2013.

The judging commitee will consist of:

  • Steve Janisse (St.Clair)
  • Lisa Symons (Conestoga)
  • Peggy Barnwell (Sheridan)

OCMC Award of Excellence

This award will be presented in recognition of a history of selfless contribution to the continuance and success of the Ontario Colleges' Marketing Competition.

Recipients may be:

  • an individual
  • a college
  • a sponsor or another organization

Please use the Nomination Form found below, and send nominations and any attachments in word format only please:

Download OCMC Award of Excellence Nomination Form

Please forward your nominations to one of the following via email:

  • Nicole Rourke (St. Clair) Nrouke@stclaircollege.ca
  • Peggy Barnwell (Sheridan) peggy.barnwell@sheridanc.on.ca

Please submit all nominations by Friday November 1, 2013.

The Recipients of the 2012 Lydiatt Award and the Award of Excellence will be announced at the Gala Awards Banquet on Friday, November 22, 2013.
